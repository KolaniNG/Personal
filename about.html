About Me!

My name is Jo Guzman, but I go by the name KOLANI online.
I'm an artist, animator, voice actor and generally just a creative guy.

I've worked mainly in independent online animation and game projects. Here's a list! For examples of just my work, you can take a look around my website's art and animation pages. :)

Animation Projects

  • Girls Night - Served as a rough animator for a couple shots in a student film.
  • FulpWare - Animated a short segment in this celebration of Newgrounds.com
  • Pico And The Party Crashers - Short action cartoon which was a collaboration between myself and LobsterMango primarily. I handled all the scenes where Pico (the kid with the orange hair) was fighting on his own. I also too care of the backgrounds.

Games Projects

  • POLY POLY POLY - A special puzzle game designed and programmed by my friend Dusky. I designed and animated the little tutorial square character, logo and I also drew all the abstract backgrounds used in the game.
  • Ruby Heart Matchmakers - Another match puzzle game made for a jam. I didn't have as much time to contribute art to this, but what I did was mainly the character animation for the Gamer VS Weeb, and Revolution stages. I was the one who came up with the main premise of two gods helping bring people together through the power of love. I also designed the logo here.
